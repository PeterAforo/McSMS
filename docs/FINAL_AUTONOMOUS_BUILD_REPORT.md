### **HR & Payroll API:**

**Employees:**
- `GET /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=employees` - List employees
- `GET /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=employees&id={id}` - Employee details
- `POST /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=employees` - Add employee
- `PUT /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=employees&id={id}` - Update employee

**Payroll:**
- `GET /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=payroll&action=generate&month={YYYY-MM}` - Generate payroll
- `GET /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=payroll&action=by_month&month={YYYY-MM}` - Monthly payroll
- `GET /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=payroll&action=by_employee&employee_id={id}` - Employee payroll history
- `GET /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=payroll&id={id}` - Payroll details
- `PUT /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=payroll&id={id}&action=process` - Process payroll
- `PUT /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=payroll&id={id}&action=pay` - Mark as paid

**Leave:**
- `GET /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=leave&action=pending` - Pending applications
- `GET /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=leave&action=by_employee&employee_id={id}` - Employee leaves
- `POST /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=leave` - Apply for leave
- `PUT /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=leave&id={id}&action=approve` - Approve leave
- `PUT /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=leave&id={id}&action=reject` - Reject leave

**Attendance:**
- `GET /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=attendance&action=by_date&date={date}` - Daily attendance
- `GET /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=attendance&action=by_employee&employee_id={id}&month={YYYY-MM}` - Employee attendance
- `POST /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=attendance` - Mark attendance

**Performance:**

- `GET /api/hr_payroll.php?resource=performance&id={id}` - Review details
